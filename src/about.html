03 : About the Story

PLOT OF THE STORY

The story begins with a duckling that is shunned and ridiculed by other animals due to its perceived ugliness. As it grows, the duckling faces various challenges, including rejection, mockery, and harsh weather. However, the narrative takes a transformative turn when the duckling discovers its true identity as a beautiful swan, finding acceptance and happiness among its kind.

CLIMAX OF THE STORY

The climax occurs when the once-ugly duckling, now transformed into a stunning swan, sees its reflection in the water and realizes its true beauty. This moment represents a powerful revelation and a turning point in the duckling's life, shifting from a sense of rejection and inadequacy to self-discovery and acceptance.

LESSON FROM THE STORY

The story of the ugly duckling imparts a valuable lesson about self-acceptance and the transformative power of time. It encourages readers to embrace individual differences, as what may seem like a disadvantage or ugliness can evolve into something beautiful and fulfilling. The narrative promotes the idea that everyone has their unique journey and potential for positive transformation.
